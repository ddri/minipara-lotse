## Companies

### Alice&Bob

Developing fault-tolerant quantum computers using cat qubits to reduce error rates.

- https://alice-bob.com/


### Alpine Quantum Technologies

Focuses on trapped-ion quantum computing for industrial applications.

- https://www.aqt.eu/


### Atom Computing

Building highly scalable quantum computers using optically trapped neutral atoms.

https://atom-computing.com/


### AWS Braket
https://aws.amazon.com/braket/
Amazon's quantum computing service providing access to quantum hardware from various providers.

### Bleximo
https://bleximo.com/
Develops superconducting quantum processors for specific industry applications.

### ColdQuanta
https://coldquanta.com/
Specializes in cold atom technology for quantum computing and sensing.

### D-Wave Systems
https://www.dwavesys.com/
Pioneers in quantum annealing technology, offering quantum computers and software solutions for optimization problems.

### Google Quantum AI
https://quantumai.google/
Developing quantum processors and algorithms, with a focus on quantum supremacy and error correction.

### IBM Quantum
https://www.ibm.com/quantum-computing/
Offers cloud-based quantum computing services, develops quantum hardware, and provides educational resources.

### IonQ
https://ionq.com/
Specializes in trapped-ion quantum computing technology, offering both hardware and cloud access.

### Microsoft Azure Quantum
https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/solutions/quantum-computing/
Provides a cloud-based quantum computing platform and is developing topological qubits.

### OQC (Oxford Quantum Circuits)
https://oxfordquantumcircuits.com/
Develops superconducting quantum computers with a unique coaxmon qubit architecture.

### PASQAL
https://www.pasqal.io/
Builds quantum processors based on neutral atoms in optical arrays for various applications.

### PsiQuantum
- https://psiquantum.com/
Focusing on building a large-scale silicon photonic quantum computer for commercial applications.

### Q-CTRL
- https://q-ctrl.com/
Provides quantum control engineering solutions to improve quantum hardware performance.

### Quantinuum

- https://www.quantinuum.com/
Offers end-to-end quantum solutions, including hardware, software, and applications.

### Quantum Brilliance

- https://quantumbrilliance.com/
Develops room-temperature diamond-based quantum accelerators.

### Quantum Machines

- https://www.quantum-machines.co/
Creates hardware and software solutions for controlling and operating quantum computers.

### QuEra Computing

- https://www.quera.com/
Develops programmable quantum simulators and computers based on neutral atom arrays.

### Qunnect

- https://www.qunnect.inc/
Focuses on developing hardware for quantum-secure communications and quantum internet.

### Rigetti Computing

- https://www.rigetti.com/
Develops superconducting quantum integrated circuits and provides cloud access to quantum processors.

### Riverlane

- https://www.riverlane.com/
Creates software and algorithms to accelerate the development of quantum computers.

### Strangeworks

- https://strangeworks.com/
Provides a quantum computing ecosystem that enables organizations to start developing quantum applications.

### Xanadu

- - https://www.xanadu.ai/
Specializes in photonic quantum computing and offers cloud-based quantum computing services.
